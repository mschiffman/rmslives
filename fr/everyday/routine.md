### 📖 <span onclick="playAudio('Note de grammaire')">Note de grammaire</span>

**Reflexive Verbs for Body Actions**

French daily routines often use **reflexive verbs**:

- <span onclick="playAudio('se réveiller')">se réveiller</span> — to wake up
- <span onclick="playAudio('se lever')">se lever</span> — to get up
- <span onclick="playAudio('se laver')">se laver</span> — to wash
- <span onclick="playAudio('se coucher')">se coucher</span> — to go to bed
- <span onclick="playAudio('s\'endormir')">s'endormir</span> — to fall asleep

**Structure:** se + verb + definite article

✅ <span onclick="playAudio('Je me lave les mains')">Je me lave les mains.</span> — I wash my hands.

❌ Je lave mes mains.

### 🔧 <span onclick="playAudio('Truc et Machin')">« Truc » et « Machin » — Words for "Thing"</span>

**truc** = thing / stuff / something — Very common in everyday conversation.
**machin** = thing / what's-it-called / thingamajig — Used when you don't remember the name.

#### Examples with « truc »

- <span onclick="playAudio('Je cherche un truc')">Je cherche un truc.</span> — I'm looking for something.
- <span onclick="playAudio('Tu peux me passer ce truc')">Tu peux me passer ce truc ?</span> — Can you pass me that thing?
- <span onclick="playAudio('C\'est quoi ce truc')">C'est quoi ce truc ?</span> — What is this thing?
- <span onclick="playAudio('Je veux un truc comme ça')">Je veux un truc comme ça.</span> — I want something like that.
- <span onclick="playAudio('C\'était un truc de fou')">C'était un truc de fou.</span> — It was crazy.

#### Examples with « machin »

- <span onclick="playAudio('Passe-moi le machin là')">Passe-moi le machin là.</span> — Pass me that thing there.
- <span onclick="playAudio('Comment s\'appelle ce machin')">Comment s'appelle ce machin ?</span> — What's that thing called?
- <span onclick="playAudio('Le machin pour ouvrir les bouteilles')">Le machin pour ouvrir les bouteilles.</span> — The thing for opening bottles.
